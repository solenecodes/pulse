# Pulse Product Copy

Write in short, premium lifestyle language. Favor sensory effect over ingredient lists.

Rules:
- Product names should be two or three words.
- Copy should feel urban, clean, and performance-led.
- Avoid medical, gym-bro, or generic supplement phrasing.
- Keep CTAs direct: Shop the line, Add, View drops.
